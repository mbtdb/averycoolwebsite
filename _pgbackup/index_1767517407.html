welcome.

this is my personal website.

Also, this is the small window to my massive backend services that run on this domain lol

i use it as a place to collect things i care about — notes, ideas, screenshots, projects, and whatever else doesn’t really fit anywhere else.

i’m not trying to make it polished or complete. I know, i've used tons of assets from others, but it's temporary, as I will slowly build this site up

what you’ll find here

some pages are more technical, some are more personal, and some are just collections of things i didn’t want to lose. there isn’t a strict structure, and that’s intentional.

i’m more interested in having a space i control than building something that looks impressive. if something feels unfinished, it probably is — and that’s fine.

about this site

most of this is written and edited by hand. things will change, break, and get rewritten over time. older versions might disappear. new sections might show up without explanation.

i like older personal websites because they felt quieter and more deliberate. this is my attempt at keeping that spirit alive.

this site doesn’t need to justify itself.
